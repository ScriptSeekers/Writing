Initial Writings

Junoon-e-Ishq
Premi apni mohabbat ko itni shiddat se jeeta hai ki woh apne jazbaat ko qurbani, shayari aur junoon ke roop mein bayan karta hai.
Tum kisi talab par aao, Main wahi doob dikhauga. Akshar bhar mera naam ka bolo, Badnaam bhi ho jaunga. ❤️ Tum se hai to duniya se pyaar hai mujhko, Tum muh fero, Main qayamat launga. Praan mere pallo mein baandhke, Bas awaaz bakhsh do. Tumhari payal aur meri dhadkan si, Main nazmon mein sunaunga. Aur jo koi sookha kar de, Meri is madhushala ko, ❤️ Main devon ke kanthon se halahal ugalvaunga.
Keemat se Pare Mohabbat
Par kismat ka vyangya — jis cheez ke liye sab kuch dena padta, woh kisi aur ko bina keemat mil gayi.
Mujhe wo har minnat me chahiye tha, Aankhon ke zeenat pe chaliye tha. Usse muft mein paa liye kisi ne… Jo mujhe har keemat mein chahiye tha.
The Lesson of First Love
Pehla pyaar chahe khatam ho jaye, par woh insaan ko hamesha badal deta hai.
No matter how it ends, first love always changes you. It always teaches you something very special, something very deep. #NewPerspective #First-Love #deep.love #LOVE_IS_ROYALTY
