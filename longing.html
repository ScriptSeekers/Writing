Ishq Ki Aag
A confession of love so intense it turns devotion into self-destruction—where the heart worships instead of choosing, and love burns more than it heals.
Maine pucha apne Ishq se, "Kya main kar sakta hoon tumhari fikar?" Ishq ne dhadkan chhoo kar kaha, "Jo tumhara dil chahe, wahi sachkar." Dil ne cheekh kar kaha, "Main tumhara hona chahta hoon… Poora, bina kisi bhi shart ke." Par main dil de na saka, Kyuki yeh bechaara dil Tumhari har ada, har nazakat ko Bhagwan ki tarah chahne laga hai. Ab halat yeh hai Mera dil bhi Ishq ki tarah Tum par hi jalta, Tum par hi pighalta, Tum par hi bikhar raha hai. Ki chandi sona ek taraf, aur tera hona ek taraf. Ek taraf teri aankhen, jaadu tona ek taraf.
Dono Ke Beech
A story of mutual feelings trapped in silence, where both hearts know the truth but fear timing more than regret.
Na tu bola, na main, par har lafz ke beech kuch था. Hawa bhi jaanti thi, ke khamoshi mein ishq sa kuch था. Tere dekhne ka andaaz, aur mera nazar jhuka lena, ye dono adhoori baatein, par dil ka keh jaana. Kisi ne poocha, “kuch chal raha hai?” hum dono muskura diye, ek jaise jazbaat the dono ke, bas alfaaz kahaan diye? Tu bhi samajhta tha, main bhi mehsoos karti thi, par dono ne khud se kaha, “abhi waqt nahi, bas yehi sahi.” Aur waqt guzarta gaya, par wo ehsaas wahi tha na izhaar hua, na inkaar, par pyaar… dono ke dilon mein wahi था. ❤️
The First Time I Saw Her
An internal battle between hope and restraint—about falling for someone slowly, honestly, and not knowing whether to risk friendship for truth.
The first time I saw her, the world slowed down for a breath. I was on the field sweat, dust, and nightlight and there she stood, in a white jacket and black jeans, composed, distant, whispering to her phone, as if the universe bowed quietly to her calm. I don't know why, but something inside me stirred that day a pull, a quiet ache. She felt like a story I could never belong to, someone far beyond my reach, a league I could only dream of entering. Four years slipped by like passing clouds, and now, she talks to me. Sometimes we walk, side by side, under evening skies, and I swear, she feels almost like my type maybe even more. I've never lied to her, not once my heart's been an open page. But hers? I can't read it yet. And now I stand here, torn should I keep this fragile friendship, or risk it all, and let love take its chance?

I'll Wait
A raw admission of emotional dependence—hopeful, loyal, but walking the thin line between faith and self-loss.
God is not letting me forget about you and me. Every single day I miss you, I feel alone without you. I don't know what is going on in my mind, I just know that you're the love of my life. So I'll wait for you, and I know that one day we will be together and live happily....
