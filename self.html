Ek Nishani
Samjho to ehsaas hoon… warna sirf ek kahani.
Rakh sako to ek nishani hu mai, Kho do to sirf ek kahani hu mai. Wo ek bund aankh ka paani hu mai. Sabko pyaar dene ki aadat hai mujhe, Apni alag pehchaan banane ki aadat hai mujhe. Kitna bhi gehra zakhm de koi, Utna hi zyada muskurane ki aadat hai mujhe. Akela khwab hu mai, Sawalon se khafa chhota sa jawab hu mai. Jo samajh na sake — unke liye kaun? Jo jaan gaye — unke liye khuli kitaab hu mai. Aankhon se dekhoge to khush paoge, Dil se puchoge to dard ka sailaab hu mai. Agar rakh sako to ek nishani hu mai, Kho do to sirf ek kahani hu mai.
Badla Hua Mai
Log kehte hain main badal gaya… sach ye hai, logon ne badal diya.
Khamosh chehre par Hazaaro pehre hote hai, Hasti aankhon me bhi Zakhm gehre hote hai. Jaroorat thi sahare ki Jab toot gaye the mere sapne, Paraayo ne saath diya, Aur rang dikha gaye apne. Bura nahi hu mai, Meri bhi kuch kahani hai, Yeh jo badla badla sa lagta hu, Apno ki hi meharbani hai.
